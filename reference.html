Frequently Asked Questions:

Why Won’t RStudio Won’t Let Me Download Packages?:

If you’re getting errors when downloading packages like:

syntax error near unexpected token `"shiny"`

you are attempting to download your packages within the RStudio terminal. To download packages for R and RStudio, ensure you’re in the console tab.

How Do I See MyFavoriteAlbums in My Browser?:

To see MyFavoriteAlbums in your browser, navigate to App.R, and click the button Run App located in the RStudio navigation bar. Clicking this button will open the local version of MyFavoriteAlbums in your browser.

You can also view MyFavoriteAlbums by accessing a link for your deployed MyFavorite Albums.

To deploy MyFavoriteAlbums, follow the instructions here.

Why is MyFavoriteAlbums Not Recognizing My Album Data?:

MyFavoriteAlbums may not be recognizing your album data because your CSV is not named properly. MyFavoriteAlbums requires album data files to be named album-rankings.csv and will not recognize your file if it is named something else. MyFavoriteAlbums also requires your data file to be stored within the /data directory and have your working directory set properly.

This warning occurs in your RStudio console when your album data file is not named properly, your album data file is not located properly, or your working directory is not set correctly:

cannot open file 'data/album-rankings.csv': No such file or directory

To rename your album data file, follow the instructions here.

To store your album data file properly, follow the instructions here.

How Do I Modify the Year Range In the Number One Albums Tab?:

To modify the year range in the Number One Albums tab, first verify your new year range by finding the year of your oldest and newest rated album. This can be done by checking out your logged data in album-rankings.csv.

Once you know the years for your albums’ year range, follow the instructions to update the year slider here.

Also make sure to modify the year range in the Artist Comparison tab, by following the instructions here.

Why Aren’t My Changes to MyFavoriteAlbums Showing Up in My Deployed Version?:

If you’ve made changes to MyFavoriteAlbums and they aren’t showing up in your deployed version of the web application:

  1. Ensure that the files where you’ve made changes have been saved in RStudio.
  2. Once all of your modified files have been saved, navigate to App.R.
  3. Click the Republish button in the RStudio navigation bar. A pop-up window will appear on your screen verifying the files to be published by Shiny for MyFavoriteAlbums.
  4. If you have not deployed MyFavoriteAlbums, you will see a button that says Publish. To deploy MyFavoriteAlbums, follow the instructions here.
  5. In the pop-up window, click publish. Within your RStudio console you will see shiny’s logs as MyFavoriteAlbums is being built. Once MyFavoriteAlbums has been successfully built, the updated version of MyFavoriteAlbums will open in a new browser window.
